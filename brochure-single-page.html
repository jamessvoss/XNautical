2026 Season & Best Dates
Peak Season: June 1 – Sept 15
Best Weather + Fishing: July 1 – Sept 1
Halibut:  Excellent May 15 – Sept 30. 2 per day, no annual limit
Rockfish:  Year-round. 3 per day (1 nonpelagic). Prized yelloweye!
Salmon:  Kings May–Aug. Silvers mid-July–Sept. Peak in summer
Lingcod:  Retention July 1+. 1 per day, 35″ minimum
The Experience
  • Depart Homer Harbor into breathtaking Kachemak Bay — glaciers, mountains & wildlife surround you
  • Spot whales, otters, seals, puffins & eagles on the way to the fishing grounds
  • Expert crew coaches you through every step — perfect for beginners to seasoned pros
  • Fish up to 4,500 square miles of some of the richest halibut waters on earth
  • Maximum 6 anglers per boat for a personalized, uncrowded experience
  • Crew fillets & bags your catch right on the boat — fish processing available at the dock
  • Fast, heated boats with restrooms, charging outlets & top-of-the-line equipment
  • Average halibut 25–40 lbs with daily shots at 100+ lb "barn doors"
