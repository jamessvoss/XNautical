Built for the water

XNautical was created by a mariner who believes every boat deserves access to professional-grade navigation tools -- without the professional price tag.

Our Mission

Making official chart data accessible to everyone

NOAA produces some of the best nautical chart data in the world, and it's all freely available to the public. But accessing that data on a mobile device -- in a format that's actually useful while underway -- has traditionally required expensive subscription software.

XNautical changes that. We take official NOAA Electronic Navigational Charts, tide predictions, current data, and marine weather and bring them together in a single, beautifully designed mobile application that works even without an internet connection.

Whether you're a weekend sailor, a commercial fisherman, a kayaker exploring the coast, or a charter captain, you deserve to navigate with the same quality data used on commercial ship bridges worldwide.

Our Values

What guides us

Safety First

Good navigation saves lives. We use only official, authoritative data sources and clearly mark that XNautical is not a replacement for primary navigation equipment and paper charts.

Official Data Only

No crowdsourced depths, no user-submitted hazards, no approximations. Every data point in XNautical comes from a U.S. government agency you can verify and trust.

Offline by Design

The ocean doesn't have Wi-Fi. XNautical is designed from the ground up to work completely offline, because that's exactly when you need navigation the most.

Crafted with Care

Every pixel, every interaction, every chart symbol is considered. We believe navigation software should be as beautiful as it is functional.

For Every Mariner

From kayakers to commercial captains, everyone on the water deserves access to professional-grade navigation tools. Price shouldn't determine safety.

Continuously Improving

XNautical is actively developed with new features, expanded coverage, and improvements based on real feedback from real mariners.
