FAQ

Frequently asked questions

XNautical is free to download and use. The NOAA chart data, tide predictions, and current data are all sourced from publicly available government data. We believe safety-critical navigation tools should be accessible to everyone on the water.

Yes! XNautical is designed for offline use. Download chart regions, tide predictions, and current data while you have internet, then use them fully offline. GPS, compass, waypoints, and all navigation features work without any internet connection.

All chart data comes from NOAA's Office of Coast Survey in the official S-57 Electronic Navigational Chart (ENC) format. Tide and current predictions come from NOAA CO-OPS, weather data from the National Weather Service and National Data Buoy Center. We use only official government data sources.

XNautical covers the entire U.S. coastline across nine NOAA chart districts: Northeast, Mid-Atlantic, Southeast, Gulf Coast, Great Lakes, Pacific Northwest, California, Alaska, and Pacific Islands (Hawaii and territories). See our Charts page for full details.

Storage varies by region. Charts are stored as efficient MBTiles vector tiles, which are much smaller than raster images. A typical coastal region might require a few hundred megabytes. You can see the exact size of each download before you start, and the app shows total storage used in the stats dashboard.

XNautical is currently available on Android. An iOS version is in development and coming soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when the iOS version launches.

XNautical is an aid to navigation, not a replacement for primary navigation equipment. While it provides comprehensive navigation data, you should always carry official paper charts and maintain proper navigation practices as required by maritime regulations. XNautical is an excellent backup and planning tool that complements your primary navigation equipment.

We regularly process and update chart data from NOAA's chart database. When you open the app with an internet connection, it can check for and download updated chart data. We recommend periodically re-downloading your regions to ensure you have the latest chart corrections and updates.
