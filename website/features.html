Features

Professional-grade marine navigation tools designed for every mariner, from weekend cruisers to commercial operators.

Charts & Navigation

Official NOAA Electronic Navigational Charts

XNautical renders S-57 ENC data with proper S-52 symbology, the international standard for electronic chart display used on commercial vessels worldwide.

  • Multi-scale chart quilting with seamless zoom transitions
  • SCAMIN filtering -- features appear at appropriate zoom levels
  • Depth areas, contours, and individual depth soundings
  • Lights, buoys, beacons, and all standard navigation aids
  • Wrecks, rocks, obstructions, and hazard markings
  • Cables, pipelines, restricted areas, and anchorages

GPS & Sensors

Precision GPS and sensor-fused compass

Real-time position tracking with detailed GPS data, plus a beautifully rendered compass with smooth heading updates using device sensor fusion.

  • Real-time position, speed, and course over ground
  • Four compass modes: full, half-moon, ticker tape, minimal
  • GNSS satellite constellation viewer with signal strength
  • GPS accuracy and fix quality indicators

Tides & Currents

1,500+ stations with detailed predictions

Plan your day on the water with precision. View tide heights and current velocities with beautiful interactive charts spanning the next 36 hours.

  • 578 tide stations with high/low predictions and curves
  • 937 current stations with velocity and direction data
  • 36-hour interpolated tide and current charts
  • Map markers with visual fill-level indicators
  • All predictions available offline once downloaded
Tide predictions screenshot
Marine Weather

Comprehensive weather from every angle

Multiple weather data sources give you a complete picture of marine conditions. Zone forecasts, live buoy data, wind maps, and even weather cameras.

  • NOAA marine zone forecasts with polygon map overlays
  • NDBC buoy observations: wind speed, wave height, temperature
  • Windy.com integration for wind and wave forecasts
  • FAA weather camera feeds for real-time visual conditions
  • Synopsis, period forecasts, and marine advisories
