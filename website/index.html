Nautical Charts

Official NOAA charts, rendered beautifully

XNautical renders S-57 Electronic Navigational Charts with proper S-52 symbology -- the same standard used on commercial ship bridges worldwide.

  • Multi-scale chart quilting with automatic transitions
  • Depth areas, contours, soundings, and navigation aids
  • Lights, buoys, beacons, wrecks, and obstructions
  • Download by region for complete offline access

Tides & Currents

Plan around the water, not around guesswork

Access tide and current predictions from over 1,500 NOAA stations. Beautifully rendered 36-hour curves show you exactly what the water is doing.

  • 578 tide stations with high/low predictions
  • 937 current stations with velocity and direction
  • Visual fill-level indicators on chart markers
  • Predictions cached offline -- no internet required
