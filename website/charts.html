Data Sources

Authoritative government data

XNautical sources all navigation data from official U.S. government agencies. No crowdsourced data, no approximations -- just authoritative, survey-grade information you can trust.

NOAA Office of Coast Survey
Official Electronic Navigational Charts (ENC) in S-57 format. The same data used by commercial vessels for ECDIS compliance. Updated regularly from NOAA's chart database.
NOAA CO-OPS
Center for Operational Oceanographic Products and Services. Tide and current predictions from 1,515 stations. Harmonic constituent-based predictions.
NOAA National Weather Service
Marine weather zone forecasts with detailed period forecasts, synopses, and hazard warnings. Coastal and offshore marine zones with polygon boundaries.
National Data Buoy Center
Real-time observations from weather buoys: wind speed and direction, wave height and period, air and water temperature, barometric pressure.
