The Experience

Your adventure begins at Homer Harbor as you motor into breathtaking Kachemak Bay, surrounded by glacier-filled mountains and incredible wildlife. Spot whales, otters, seals, puffins, and bald eagles before you even reach the fishing grounds.

  • Fish up to 4,500 square miles of the richest halibut waters on earth
  • Expert crew coaches you through every step — beginners to seasoned pros

Monthly Highlights

May: Season opens. Great weather, sunshine, kings, rockfish & halibut moving in from deep water. First whale sightings.

June: Peak season begins! Kings, halibut & rockfish in full swing. Whale sightings increase. Book early — fills fast.

July: Prime time. Best weather, major salmon runs, lingcod opens July 1. The very best of the year.

August: Silver salmon arrive mid-month. Fishing remains excellent. Crowds begin to thin.

September: Big fish, fewer crowds. Weather more variable — book early in your trip for flexibility.

What We Provide

All fishing gear — rods, reels, tackle & bait. Expert instruction and coaching for all skill levels. Fish filleting & bagging on the boat included. No experience necessary! Our professional, USCG-licensed crew handles everything from start to finish.

Getting Your Fish Home

We fillet & bag your catch right on the boat. At the dock, a fish processor meets you — they portion, vacuum seal, freeze, and box your fish for the trip home. Airlines are fully prepared for frozen fish boxes. Anchorage airport even has overnight freezer storage for ~$10/box.

Meeting Location & Departure

  • Homer Harbor Marina — meet at Harbor Grill kiosk
  • Check-in 6:15 AM, depart ~6:30 AM (7:00 AM September+)
  • Full-day trips return 3:30–4:30 PM
  • Staff contacts you 1–2 days before with boat & slip details

Good to Know
